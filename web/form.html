Nehru Arts And Science

college

Nehru Arts and Science College (NASC) is a prominent educational institution located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 1997, the college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various fields, including arts, science, commerce, and management. Known for its academic excellence, NASC provides a conducive learning environment, equipped with modern infrastructure, skilled faculty, and state-of-the-art facilities. The college emphasizes holistic development, encouraging students to participate in extracurricular activities, research, and community service, making it a well-rounded institution for higher education.
